Résumé

I went from a private in the Army of the United States to the most famous astronaut in the universe (Юра, прости)! In 2014 I spent six months on the International Space Station for ESA’s Blue Dot mission. I'm now back in space on my second mission called Horizons where I will take over as Station commander for Expedition 57. My mission is to pioneer the future in space exploration, scientific discovery and aeronautics research.
